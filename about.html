About Adam

Overview

Adam Kopec is a multidisciplinary self-taught designer, product thinker and entreprenuer living bicoastally between New York City and Portland. He is currently the Co-Founder and Head of Design at Oak, where he is focused on building the future of meditation.

From 2015-2018, he was the Design Director at Hodinkee, the world’s leading lifestyle brand for watch collectors, where he led a team of designers and engineers to build compelling on and offline experiences. Prior, he founded his own design studio and worked with clients like Warby Parker, Microsoft, First Round Capital and Casey Neistat.

Throughout his career, he has worked with various early, mid-stage and post-IPO technology companies as a product designer and strategist. Some time ago he graduated from Boston University with an Advertising degree.

Adam is an avid musician, traveler, vintage watch and car enthusiast. He is passionate about mental wellness and loves building human-centric products with soul.
